Privacy Policy

How the ArcaWand Soft website handles technical data, contact messages, launch notifications, analytics, external services and user rights.

Scope of this policy

This policy applies to the public ArcaWand Soft website at arcawand-soft.com, including general pages about ArcaWand Soft, public product presentation pages, contact pages, launch announcements and informational content.

Product-specific pages may have their own privacy policy. This general website policy does not describe the internal storage, permissions or feature behavior of any product or browser extension.

Who operates the website

The website is operated by ArcaWand Soft. For privacy questions, data requests, support questions or security concerns, contact contact@arcawand-soft.com.

Data processed when you visit

The website is mainly a static informational site and does not require a visitor account. Hosting, security and performance systems may automatically process technical data such as IP address or approximate location, browser and device details, requested URL, referrer, language preference, access date and time, error information and basic interaction signals.

This information is used to deliver the site, keep it available, prevent abuse, diagnose errors, measure performance, understand which pages are useful and improve content quality.

Contact messages

When you contact ArcaWand Soft by email or through a contact link, we receive the information you choose to send: email address, name if provided, message content, subject, attachments and any voluntary technical context.

We use this data to respond, provide support, manage business or press inquiries, follow up on product feedback and keep a reasonable record of exchanges when needed.

Launch notifications and email forms

Some pages may invite visitors to leave an email address to be notified about a launch or important update. When active, the address is used only for the stated notification and directly related communication.

Forms may rely on a secure server-side endpoint or email service. Private API keys and provider secrets are never intentionally published in public website code. You can unsubscribe or request deletion at any time.

Analytics, cookies and local storage

The site may use privacy-conscious analytics, security scripts, performance tools or error diagnostics. Depending on configuration, these tools may use cookies, local storage, request metadata or similar browser technologies.

Language preferences, interface choices or dismissal states may be stored locally in your browser so the site behaves consistently. You can clear cookies and site data from your browser settings.

Hosting and external services

The website is hosted as a static site and may use infrastructure services for hosting, DNS, CDN, security, analytics, email delivery, form processing, payment links and external distribution pages.

These providers process limited data only as needed to provide their service, secure the site, route messages, process payments, measure performance or comply with legal obligations. External checkout pages, app stores, Chrome Web Store pages, social platforms and third-party links are governed by their own privacy policies.

Purposes and legal bases

Depending on your location, processing may rely on legitimate interest in operating, securing and improving the website, consent where required for optional analytics or marketing communications, contractual necessity when responding to a direct request, and legal obligations where records must be kept.

Data sharing

ArcaWand Soft does not sell personal data collected through the website. Limited data may be shared with service providers that help host, secure, analyze, operate or communicate through the site, or when required for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention or legitimate legal requests.

Retention

Technical logs are kept only for a reasonable period needed for security, debugging, analytics and operational reliability. Contact emails are retained as long as needed to answer the request, maintain business records and protect legitimate interests. Notification sign-up data is kept until unsubscribe, deletion request or service cleanup.

Security

The site uses HTTPS and reputable infrastructure providers. No public website can guarantee absolute security, but the site is kept as simple and static as possible, avoids unnecessary data collection and avoids exposing private credentials in front-end code.

Your rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to processing, withdraw consent, request portability or lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact contact@arcawand-soft.com. We may need enough information to verify and process the request.

Children

The website is not directed to children and does not knowingly request personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the site, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the website, legal requirements, service providers or privacy practices change. The latest version published on this page applies from the date it is made available.

Contact

For privacy questions about the ArcaWand Soft website, email contact@arcawand-soft.com.
